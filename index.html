Who We Are

Founded by Aman Ahuja, Fenris is a network of independent practitioners who help civil society organizations use data and AI technologies effectively.

We are technologists, designers, data governance experts, and product strategists — senior practitioners who have chosen to focus their expertise where it is hardest and most needed.

We are small by design. Each member brings deep experience and works as a peer, not a subcontractor. We assemble the right team for each engagement, drawing from the network as the work demands.

How We Work

  1. We are a values-driven consultant network. We care about who we work with and how. We care about the impact our work and our clients have in the world.

  2. Our clients are the experts. Your organization and its stakeholders understand the problem space better than we ever will. We listen and learn before we propose, and center your team’s excellence.

  3. We build for the long term. Governance, ownership, and sustainability are built into every engagement, not added later when something breaks.

  4. We build capacity, not dependency. The goal of every engagement is to leave your team stronger and more capable than when we arrived.

  5. We stay alongside you. After the deep work is done, we provide a flexible advisory and mentoring period — usually working with the product lead — to support continuity, follow-through, and to learn from the work over time.
